All About The Owl House

Accidentally sent to the world of the Boiling Isles before a trip to summer camp, a teenage human named Luz longs to become a witch. She is aided by rebellious Eda the Owl Lady and a pint-sized demon named King. Throughout her journey in the Boiling Isles, she learns valuable lessons about life, love, loss, and being her true self.

Rating: TV-Y7-FV

Genre: Fantasy

Production Company: Disney Television Animation

Writers: Dana Terrace, Molly Knox Ostertag

Release Date: January 2020

End Date: April 2023
